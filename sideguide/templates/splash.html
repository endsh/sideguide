The Team

We are young developers, designers, researchers, and entrepeneurs. Our goal is to replace paper maps and navigation signs and improve traffic flow, by creating a well-built and easy-to-use app with seamless design and smart AI.

Future goals include creating a web portal for visitors to experience virtual tours of destinations, providing open APIs for developers to incorporate open collections into applications, and building game modes and achievements that make trips educational and fun for children... and adults.
