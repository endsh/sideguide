The Team

We are young developers, designers, researchers, and entrepeneurs. Our goal is to replace paper maps and navigation signs while improving traffic flow, by creating a well-built and easy-to-use application with seamless design, smart artificial intelligence, and useful visualizations.

Future goals include creating a web portal for visitors to experience virtual tours of destinations, providing APIs for incorporating open collections into existing applications, and building game modes and achievements that make trips educational and fun for children... and adults.

Currently looking for venture capital to quit our day jobs and make this happen. Give us a shot by scheduling a meeting
